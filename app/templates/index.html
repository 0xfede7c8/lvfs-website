Linux Vendor
Firmware Service

The Linux Vendor Firmware Service is a secure portal which allows hardware vendors to upload firmware updates. Files can be uploaded privately and optionally embargoed until a specific date.

This site is used by all major Linux distributions to provide metadata for clients such as fwupdmgr and GNOME Software. To upload firmware please login, or request a new account. There is no charge to vendors for the hosting or distribution of content.
